What is LEGO Island?

LEGO Island is one of the fisrt LEGO video games ever created. It was released in October of 1997, and was developed by Mindscape. It's success lead to two sequels (LEGO Island 2: The Brickster's Revenge and Island Xtreme Stunts), as well as a theme of LEGO sets. This fan site will focus primarily on the first game.

Rest in peace Wes Jenkins May 6, 1952 – September 24, 2017. Thanks for the memories!
