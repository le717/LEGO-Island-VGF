News Footage

Lorin Tone (one of the composers of the game's music) has uploaded a fascinating video from the development of LEGO Island. At the time, it was still called "Adventures on LEGO Island". Dr. Paul Melmed is interviewed, and footage is shown of kids playing with the life-sized model of the island, as well as a few of the big-name actors in the recording booth.


You can see that the intro is playing back twice as fast. The intro has temporary voice over, so it seems like this faster speed was what was originally intended. Maybe they slowed it down because they were afraid it would make kids motion sick?
