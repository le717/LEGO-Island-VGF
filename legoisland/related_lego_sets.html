Sets Related to LEGO Island

  • 6337: Fast Track Finish – Used for the race track. Modified: grandstands were added; a few parts, like the track map were removed.
  • 6335: Indy Transport – Only the Formula 1 race cars were used.
  • 6546: Slick Racer – An additional Formula 1 race car.
  • 6598: Metro PD Station – Used for the Police Station, Helicopter, and Jail. Modified: the backside of the set is open, so a back was built for the game; the police sign & dock it's sitting on were removed.
  • 6334: Wave Jump Racers – Used the lifeguard tower, jet skis, and buoys. The buggy was not used
  • 6517: Water Jet – Self-explanitory
  • 6518: Baja Buggy – This is the dune buggy you can build in the game.
  • 6500: Holiday Village – This LEGO set was never released, but contained the Super Store and Pizzeria logos, as well as the 3 panel window frames in blue, which were only available in one other set.
  • 6340: Hook & Ladder – This fire truck can be seen in the winning cutscene, as is a roadblock on the Speedway race track.
  • 6339: Shuttle Launch Pad – Thie shuttle can be seen during Formula 1 races advertising for the Pizzeria.
  • 2181: Infomaniac – This minifigure was only packaged with the earliest copies of the game.
  • 6410: Cabana Beach – A recolored version of the fishing speedboat can be seen during the Jet Ski races.
  • 6338: Hurricane Harbour – The boxart for this set is used for all three boxes in the score cube room. This is the only officially released LEGO set to contain a three-paneled blue window frame. These blue window frames appear all around the island, so building a replica of the island in real life is near impossible due to the rarity of this piece.
