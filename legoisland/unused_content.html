Unused Content

By digging in the game files, audio has been extracted that helps reveal some of the unfinished, or scrapped ideas in LEGO Island

  • There is an unused outro video and accompanying music in the game's files. Most likely, this video would have played after exiting the game, before the credits.
  • There are two additional unused hat jingles for the Super Store. They contain the same lyrics as the jingle used in the final game, but have different melodies.
  • Saving LEGO Island was initally much more complex. It seems that saves would have needed to be manual, instead of automatic. The blue sign-in book was originally much more complex, containing reg/green brick choices to do things like delete users, and warn you if you were going to overwrite another user.
